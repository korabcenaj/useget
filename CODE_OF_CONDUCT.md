# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in the useget project should remain respectful, constructive, and professional.

Contributors are expected to:

- communicate clearly and respectfully
- focus feedback on code, behavior, and outcomes
- assume good intent while still being direct about technical concerns
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, and disruptive behavior

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, and review comments.
- Keep technical disagreement factual and actionable.
- Accept feedback and course correction without hostility.
- Avoid spam, trolling, or intentionally derailing discussion.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or intimidation
- Discriminatory or hateful language
- Personal insults or sustained hostility
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the repository owner instead of opening a public issue.
